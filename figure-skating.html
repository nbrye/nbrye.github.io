Outside of academics and professional life, I am a competitive figure skater of seventeen years. I am currently an active member of the United States Figure Skating Association, and a collegiate member of the San Diego Figure Skating Club.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • (2023) Served as the competition chairperson for 2023 Ride the Tide, a US Figure Skating Sanctioned Intercollegiate competition. Accomodated over 200 competitiors from 6 different states across the Pacific Coast, recruited officials, coordinated awards, signage, volunteers, hospitality food and lodging, budgeted/managed finances, and fundraised ~$4.5k for Figure Skating @ UC San Diego. A huge thank you to the San Diego Ice Arena and the San Diego Figure Skating Club, as well as all of the officials, volunteers, board members, and skaters who were pivotal in making this event a success.
  • (2019-2023) Served four years on the Figure Skating @ UC San Diego board. During my first two years on the board as the treasurer, I handled funding requests, reimbursements, fundraiser coordination, and budgeting. During my second two years on the board as the president, I oversaw all competitive, social, and fundraising events, handled competition registration for the team, organized / delegated responsibilities at board meetings, answered team inquiries, coordinated apparel purchases, and choreographed programs for interested competitors.
  • (2018-2023) Competed in a grand total of fourteen intercollegiate figure skating competitions, in a variety of freeskate, short program, pattern dance, solo free dance events. Earned a total of 18 first place finishes, 8 second place finishes, and 5 third place finishes in individual events. Was a member of Figure Skating @ UC San Diego's national qualifying team in 2019, 2020, and 2023.
  • (2022) Passed the US Figure Skating Senior Freeskate test with honors, earning a US Figure Skating Double Gold Medalist distinction.
  • (2021-2022) Competed with and served as one of the team captains of ICEDMND, the first and only Theatre on Ice team based in San Diego. Assisted with the team's competition registration/logistics as well as music uploads, and contributed to the team's sixth place finish at the 2022 National Theatre on Ice Championships.
  • (2014-2020) Worked on and off as a junior coach, in which I assisted with Skating School classes teaching young children (and adults) how to figure skate.
  • (2019) Won the silver medal in the Excel Junior Ladies event at the Intercollegiate National Figure Skating Championships.
  • (2015) Passed the US Figure Skating Senior Moves in the Field Test, earning a US Figure Skating Gold Medalist distinction.
  • Fun Facts: While I am no longer formally junior coaching, you may find me from time to time assisting with the instruction of Skating School classes at the San Diego Ice Arena (when other coaches request a cover). You can also find me at almost every local competition either volunteering or cheering on friends, and I also assist other skaters with choreography and music cutting on occasion. My figure skating goals for the future are to continue figure skating into adulthood, and/or to potentially become an official.
